what's PGL?

PGL provides a free and simple solution to match bookings and provides for direct communications and payment between group and golf professional.No middlemen.Try it. Enjoy the experience!

Free Solution

You just book the lessons and options you need with PGL – checkout the advice page – and invite your friends, family and/or business relations to join you! And then this free and simple solution matches your booking with a coach. From then on you will have direct communication with your coach.
